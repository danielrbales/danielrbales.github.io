Dan's Famous Smores

Awesome Recipe

First a little about the author of this recipe. He has an affinity for the outdoors and loves a good campfire/firepit with friends. He started this website in hopes of inspiring others to experience the outdoors and try some new things. On to the recipe!


The following are a list of ingredients you will need for this recipe

  1. Graham crackers(Honey maid is ideal)
  2. Marshmallows (preferably stay puft ;)
  3. Bars of Chocolate (Hershey's is most common but if you are feeling fancy Ghirardelli or Lindt make amazing chocolate)

    4. For a realy indulgent treat without the need for a fire read till the end ;D

Cooking and assembly instructions

  1. Prepare your fire as you desire
  2. Heat Marshmallow over fire with a long sharpened stick (preferably until the outside is getting crispy and the inside is becoming gooey)
  3. Place the cooked marshmallow onto 1/2 of a graham cracker
  4. Place 1/2 of the chocolate bar onto the marshmallow
  5. Place the other 1/2 graham cracker on top of the chocolate and press down firmly

Congrats! you have just made your first smore




As promised... a special treat with no heat
  • Replace graham crackers with chocolate graham crackers
  • Replace chocolate bar with Reese's peanut butter cups
  • Replace marshmallow with marshmallow creme
